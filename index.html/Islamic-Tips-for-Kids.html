Islamic Tips for Kids

Say Bismillah

Encourage children to say "Bismillah" before eating or starting anything.

Pray 5 Times

Teach kids the importance of Salah and start with small prayers together.

Be Kind

Remind children to be kind to others, as kindness is loved in Islam.

Read Quran

Introduce small Surahs from the Quran and encourage memorization slowly.

Say Alhamdulillah

Teach kids to be grateful and say "Alhamdulillah" for every blessing.

Assalamu Alaikum

Say Assalamu Alaikum when meeting others..

Family and friends

Be kind to family and friends.

Fighting

Avoid fighting or hurting others

Hurting

Avoid fighting or hurting others

Sorry

Say Sorry (Astaghfirullah) when you make a mistake.

Dua

Recite Dua when waking up.

Bad habits

Avoid bad habits like lying, teasing, or being rude.

Thankful

Be thankful for food, toys, and blessings.

Truthfully

Speak truthfully and avoid lying.

Salah

Perform Salah (prayers) regularly, even short ones for kids.

Wash

Wash hands before and after eating.

Charity

Give small charity or help the needy.

Animal

Respect animals and nature.

Polite

Be polite to teachers and elders.

Smile

Smile and greet people politely
